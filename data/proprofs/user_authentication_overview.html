Keeping Your Account Safe

Your account security is important to us! This guide explains how we protect your information and what you can do to keep your account secure.

Logging In Securely

You'll need your email address and password to log in. Here's how to keep your password safe:

  • Choose a strong password: Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Don't share your password: Keep it secret!
  • Keep your password updated: Change it regularly.

Two-Step Verification (Highly Recommended!)

We strongly recommend using two-step verification for extra security. It adds a second layer of protection. Here's how it works:

  1. You enter your password as usual.
  2. We send a code to your phone (via text or an app).
  3. You enter that code to finish logging in.

Even if someone knows your password, they can't log in without that code from your phone!

You can turn it on in your account settings, under "Security."

What if I forget my Password?

Click on "Forgot Password", and follow the prompts.

Why is this important?

Two-step verification makes it much harder for anyone to access your account without your permission.
