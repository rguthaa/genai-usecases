Keeping Our Platform Updated

We regularly update our platform to bring you new features, bug fixes, and performance improvements. Here's what you should know:

Regular Improvements

We release updates regularly to make our platform even better for you.

Minimal Interruptions

We try to make updates as smoothly as possible, often during off-peak times.

How Will I Know When Updates Happen?

We'll announce major updates in advance on our Status Page and on social media. Smaller updates usually happen automatically.

What Happens During an Update?

You might experience a brief moment of slowness while we update. We'll keep these interruptions to a minimum.

Why am i getting logged out? Sometimes during updates, a log out may occur for upmost security

What happens to my tasks during the update All tasks will remain the same

We're Always Improving

We're committed to providing a great service that keeps getting better!
